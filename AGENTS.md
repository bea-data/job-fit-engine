# Project Guidance

Use the existing rule-based evaluation flow in `job_fit_engine` and keep changes consistent with the current scoring model.

## Evaluation Rules

- Prefer employer day-to-day duties, role summaries, essential requirements, and explicit support statements over apprenticeship-provider boilerplate or training-course content.
- Treat stakeholder load as `RED` only when there is repeated, central evidence that stakeholder-facing work is core to the role, such as business partnering, workshops, direct requirements gathering from multiple non-technical teams, presentation or persuasion ownership, or relationship management.
- Treat isolated collaboration language like `stakeholders`, `communicate`, `collaborative`, or `influence` as insufficient on its own for stakeholder `RED`, especially in junior technical roles.
- Classify explicit route-based entry requirements such as `experience or qualification or apprenticeship` as `Conditional` rather than `Unclear`.
- Distinguish open-entry junior roles from junior-but-filtered roles. A junior title alone does not imply graduate-only or student-only targeting, but explicit year, recency, or enrolment filters should still affect eligibility.

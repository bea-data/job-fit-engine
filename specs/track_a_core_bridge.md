# Track A: Core + Bridge

## Definition
Track A includes roles that either:
- directly move me toward my long-term technical path (Core), or
- act as credible, compounding bridge roles toward that path (Bridge)

A role must:
- support a specialist, technical, low-social-load IC trajectory
- be survivable at my current level
- not introduce reputational or environmental risk

If a role fails Track A, it should be evaluated under Track B (Buffer).

---

# Scoring System Overview

Total score: 100 points  
15 categories with fixed weights

---

## Category 1 — Core career-path alignment (Weight: 12)

Does this role move me toward:
- backend / systems / platform
- QA / data quality engineering
- structured technical IC work

Green:
- directly aligned with target or strong adjacent lane

Amber:
- useful bridge, but mixed alignment

Red:
- pushes toward analyst, stakeholder-heavy, or socially driven work

---

## Category 2 — Technical systems fit (Weight: 8)

Does the daily work involve:
- systems
- structured logic
- validation / correctness
- technical problem-solving

Green:
- real systems work, correctness-driven

Amber:
- some systems work, diluted

Red:
- mostly coordination, reporting, or business translation

---

## Category 3 — Barrier to entry (Weight: 6)

Does the role’s entry threshold match my current level?

Green:
- clearly junior, strong match

Amber:
- stretch but plausible

Red:
- expects skills I do not yet have commercially

---

## Category 4 — Day-1 usefulness (Weight: 6)

Can I provide useful value early?

Green:
- immediate contribution via validation, QA, structured work

Amber:
- partial early contribution

Red:
- usefulness depends on skills I do not yet have

---

# Track A Decision Rule

Apply only if:

- Total score ≥ 70
- No critical red flags
- Role does not violate:
  - ramp-up realism
  - psychological safety
  - stakeholder load limits

Score bands:
- 80–100 → Apply immediately
- 70–79 → Apply
- <70 → Reject from Track A

---

# Reroute Rule

If a role fails Track A:

→ Re-evaluate under Track B (Buffer)

Only keep if:
- safe
- stable
- non-damaging
